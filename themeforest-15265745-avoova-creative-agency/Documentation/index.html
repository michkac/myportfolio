HOW TO EDIT PSD FILES

 

In order to edit the file, you need to have Adobe Photoshop CS6 or later installed on your system, and the fonts mentioned in the credits installed. As you can see on the bottom, the layers are well organized and gropued into folders, which make editing very easy. So, all you have to do, is select the PSD file you want to edit, open it with Adobe Photoshop, and then select the layer at the right bottom.

That's all! Enjoy.
